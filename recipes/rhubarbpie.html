Rhubarb Pie

This Rhubarb pie was a recipe given to me by my grandfather "Jampa." He made this pie for me growing up and became a personal favorite dessert.

I am creating this recipe site to share the greatest rhubarb pie with the whole world! Enjoy the experiences along the way!

Ingredients

  • Strawberries
  • Rhubarb
  • Sugar
  • Flour
  • Water
  • Eggs
  • Vegetable Oil

Steps

  1. Assemble the ingredients
  2. Preheat the oven to 400F
  3. Work out the flour and water to a dough
  4. Place the ingredients into the dough, dont forget a pie dish dummy.
  5. Bake the pie in the oven for 45 minutes, add time if needed.
  6. Let pie cool when finished for 1 hour.
