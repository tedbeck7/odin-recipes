Spaghetti

Spaghetti is one of the most basic yet vital dinners for the everyday person. This recipe was built with convenience, cost, and flavor all in mind.

Expect a cooktime of around 60 minutes with prep and cleanup included.

Ingredients

  • Choice of Noodles
  • Spaghetti Sauce
  • Ground Turkey
  • Olive Oil
  • Water
  • Salt
  • Bread

Steps

  1. Assemble the ingredients
  2. Bring water to a boil, add noodles and boil until soft.
  3. While noodles boil, preheat olive oil in a deep pan/skillet
  4. Brown meat in pan. Once browned, drain or clean excess oil from pan.
  5. Add spaghetti sauce to the pan and bring to simmering boil, cover and let simmer for 10 minutes.
  6. Strain noodles and serve meat sauce over noodles.
